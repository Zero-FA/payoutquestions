EOD Performance Accounts (PA)
EOD PA at a Glance:
No intraday trailing drawdown  

EOD Drawdown calculated once per day at market close and enforced intraday

Daily Loss Limit (DLL) enforced intraday  

Tier Based Scaling

100% Payout Split (upon meeting payout eligibility requirements)

The End-of-Day (EOD) Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account awarded after you pass the EOD Evaluation. It is issued in the same size as the Evaluation account you passed and follows the same End-of-Day drawdown structure.

EOD PA Trading Rules
To keep your EOD Performance Account active, you must follow these rules:

1. Do Not Breach the EOD Drawdown
Your account balance, including unrealized PnL, may never touch or fall below the End-of-Day (EOD) Drawdown threshold. If it does, all positions are automatically liquidated and the PA is permanently closed.

2. No Prohibited Trading Activity
All trading must follow Apex Trader Funding risk and conduct guidelines.

Engaging in prohibited activities, including hedging violations or any form of rule circumvention, will result in immediate account closure.

FAQs
How many PAs can I have?
You may hold up to 20 PAs active at the same time. This cap applies across all PA accounts combined, whether different size, EOD, or Intraday Trailing Drawdown.

Is the Performance Account a live account?
No, the Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account.

Does hitting the Daily Loss Limit close my PA?
No. Hitting the DLL pauses trading for the remainder of the session. The account remains active and resets at the next session open.

Can my position size increase?
Yes. Position size increases as you move up Scaling Levels based on your End-of-Day balance, up to the account’s maximum level.

Do I keep 100% of approved payouts?
Yes. Approved payouts are issued at a 100% payout split once payout eligibility requirements are met.
