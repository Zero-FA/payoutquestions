EOD Performance Accounts (PA)

Overview
The End-of-Day (EOD) Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account awarded after passing an EOD Evaluation.

The Performance Account is issued in the same account size and type as the Evaluation that was passed and follows the same End-of-Day drawdown structure.


EOD Performance Account at a Glance
Key characteristics of EOD Performance Accounts:

- No intraday trailing drawdown
- End-of-Day (EOD) Drawdown calculated once per day at market close
- EOD Drawdown enforced in real time during the trading session
- Daily Loss Limit (DLL) enforced intraday
- Tier-based scaling structure
- 100% payout split after meeting payout eligibility requirements

EOD Performance Account Trading Rules

Rule 1 — Do Not Breach the EOD Drawdown
Your account balance, including unrealized PnL, may never touch or fall below the End-of-Day (EOD) Drawdown threshold.

If the threshold is reached:

- All open positions are automatically liquidated
- The Performance Account is permanently closed

See: EOD Drawdown Explained


Rule 2 — No Prohibited Trading Activity
All trading activity must follow Apex Trader Funding risk and conduct guidelines.

Engaging in prohibited trading behavior, including hedging violations or any attempt to circumvent rules, will result in immediate account closure.

See: Prohibited Activities


Frequently Asked Questions

How many Performance Accounts can I have?
You may hold up to 20 Performance Accounts at the same time.

This limit applies across all Performance Accounts combined, including different account sizes, End-of-Day accounts, and Intraday Trailing Drawdown accounts.


Is the Performance Account a live account?
No. The Performance Account is a Simulated Funded (Sim Funded) account.


Does hitting the Daily Loss Limit close my Performance Account?
No. Reaching the Daily Loss Limit pauses trading for the remainder of the trading session.

The account remains active and trading resumes at the next session reset.


Can my position size increase?
Yes. Position size can increase as you progress through the scaling tiers based on your End-of-Day account balance, up to the maximum contract limit for the account.


Do I keep 100% of approved payouts?
Yes. Approved payouts are issued at a 100% payout split once payout eligibility requirements are met.
