Connection Guide for NinjaTrader Using Rithmic

Overview
This guide explains how to set up NinjaTrader and connect it to Rithmic in order to access Apex Trader Funding accounts.

Apex accounts operate in simulation mode, so NinjaTrader must always be launched in Simulation mode when connecting.


Creating a NinjaTrader Account

Step 1 — Register
Go to:
https://account.ninjatrader.com/register

Enter your email address and click Sign Up.

Important:
Do NOT use “Sign Up with Google” or “Sign Up with Apple”.  
You must manually enter your email address to receive the setup confirmation link.

Disable any ad blockers or pop-up blockers during this process.

If you encounter issues, try using another browser such as Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.


Step 2 — Confirm Email
A confirmation screen will appear asking you to verify your email address.

Check your email inbox and click the Confirm Email Address link.


Step 3 — Create Login Credentials
Create your NinjaTrader Username and Password.

Read and agree to the required documents, then click Create User.

Important:
Save your username and password. You will need them later when logging into the NinjaTrader desktop application.


Step 4 — Start the Platform Setup
You will be redirected to the Get Started page.

Click:
Try Risk-Free Simulated Trading


Step 5 — Enter Basic Information
Enter the following information:

First Name  
Last Name  
Phone Number

Then click Continue.


NinjaTrader Dashboard

Step 6 — Access the Dashboard
You will arrive at the NinjaTrader dashboard.

If a “Choose Your Platform” popup appears, simply close the window.


Step 7 — Download NinjaTrader
Download the most recent version of the NinjaTrader platform.

Install the platform once the download completes.


Adjusting NinjaTrader Settings

Step 8 — Configure Preferences
While NinjaTrader is downloading:

Click the user icon in the top-right corner of the NinjaTrader dashboard.

Select Settings.

Go to Preferences and select Numeric as your number format.


Launching NinjaTrader

Step 9 — Log Into NinjaTrader
Open the NinjaTrader application.

Enter the username and password you created earlier.

You must enter these credentials every time you start the NinjaTrader platform.


Step 10 — Launch the Console
After logging in, NinjaTrader will display the NinjaTrader Console.

If you see a Live or Simulation option, always choose Simulation.

Apex accounts operate only in simulation mode.


Enable Multi-Provider

Step 11 — Configure Multi-Provider Mode
Inside NinjaTrader:

Go to:
Tools → Options → General

Enable the Multi-provider checkbox.

Click Apply → OK.

Restart NinjaTrader after enabling this option.


Configuring the Rithmic Connection

Step 12 — Open Connection Settings
After restarting NinjaTrader:

Go to:
Connections → Configure


Step 13 — Add Rithmic Connection
Inside the Configure window:

Click Add.

Select:
My Rithmic for NinjaTrader Brokerage

Enter the required connection details.

Click OK to save the connection.


Step 14 — Connect to Rithmic
After the connection is configured:

Go to:
Connections → My Rithmic for NinjaTrader Brokerage

Select the connection to connect to your Apex Rithmic account.

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues
If you upgraded an existing NinjaTrader installation:

Open the Connections panel and verify that all connections are visible.

If new connections cannot be created, repeat the setup steps above.


System Performance
For best performance, make sure your system drivers are up to date.

Important drivers include:

NVIDIA GPU drivers  
AMD GPU drivers


Sniper Trading System Error Fix

If you upgraded NinjaTrader and encounter a “String format” chart error when using the Sniper Trading System:

Step 1
Log into:
https://account.ninjatrader.com

Step 2
Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.

Step 3
Select Settings.

Step 4
Open the Preferences tab.

Select Numeric Symbol Format.

Step 5
Log out of the dashboard.

Step 6
Close and restart NinjaTrader.

Step 7
Reconnect to your Rithmic or NinjaTrader accounts and reopen your charts.

The error should now be resolved.


Important OCO Order Warning

OCO (One Cancels Other) orders used with Rithmic are stored locally on your computer.

This means:

NinjaTrader must remain connected to your broker for OCO orders to function correctly.

If NinjaTrader becomes disconnected and one order is filled, the other order will NOT be automatically cancelled.
