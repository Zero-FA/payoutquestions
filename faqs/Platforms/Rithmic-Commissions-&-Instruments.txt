How Commissions Are Deducted
Please review the image below that shows the Rithmic Performance Tab and how commissions are deducted.

Example: A trade is placed using 1 contract on NQ. The per side commission is $1.99. The trade is opened with 1 contract and closed with a second contract to flatten the position. Hence, 2 contracts multiplied by $1.99 commission = $3.98.


On the Rithmic Performance Tab, the trade gross P&L is shown in green, and the commissions for the trade are added in orange. This results in a P&L of -$30, then deducting $3.98 in commissions, this equals a net P&L of -$33.98.



As you execute trades, commissions are automatically deducted from your account balance. It’s important to note that:
