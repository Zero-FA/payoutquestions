Rithmic Commissions & Instruments

Overview
This guide explains the commission structure and available trading instruments for Apex Trader Funding accounts using the Rithmic platform.

Commissions apply to both Evaluation and Performance Accounts.


How Commissions Are Deducted

Commissions are charged per side of a trade.

Example:
If you trade 1 contract of NQ and the commission is $1.99 per side:

Opening the trade = $1.99  
Closing the trade = $1.99  

Total commission = $3.98 round turn.

Example calculation:

1 contract opened + 1 contract closed  
2 sides × $1.99 commission = $3.98


Performance Tab Example

In the RTrader Performance tab:

- Gross P&L is displayed in green
- Commission cost is displayed in orange
- Net P&L reflects profit or loss after commissions

Example:

Trade P&L: -$30  
Commission: -$3.98  

Final Net P&L = -$33.98


Important Commission Notes

Commissions are automatically deducted from your account balance when trades are executed.

NinjaTrader may not always display the commission deduction unless commissions are configured inside NinjaTrader.

For the most accurate balance information, check your balance inside RTrader.


Adding Commissions to NinjaTrader

To display commissions inside NinjaTrader when using Rithmic:

Follow the NinjaTrader commission configuration tutorial.

For best results, use the “Lifetime” commission template.

Rithmic Instrument List

The following instruments are currently supported for trading on Apex Rithmic accounts.

If you attempt to trade an unsupported instrument, the order will be rejected without penalty.


How to Check Available Instruments

1. Log in to your RTrader account.
2. From the Trader Dashboard, click on your account name.
3. Select View Risk Parameters.
4. A list of allowed instruments for your account will appear.

If a required instrument is missing from your list, submit a Help Desk ticket to request access.


Crypto Contract Note

Micro cryptocurrency contracts behave like mini contracts when calculating maximum contract limits.

Example:

If your account allows:

100 Micro contracts  
10 Mini contracts  

You can trade only 10 contracts of:

MBT (Micro Bitcoin)  
MET (Micro Ethereum)
