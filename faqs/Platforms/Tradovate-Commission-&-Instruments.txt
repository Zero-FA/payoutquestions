Tradovate Commission & Instruments

Overview
This guide explains the commission structure and supported trading instruments for Apex Trader Funding accounts using the Tradovate platform.

Understanding commissions and available instruments helps traders manage costs and avoid rejected trades.


How Commissions Are Deducted

Tradovate commissions are automatically deducted from your account balance when a trade is executed.

To track commission adjustments accurately, monitor your balance directly inside the Tradovate platform.

Example:

A trade is placed using 1 contract on NQ.

Per-side commission: $1.55

Opening the position: $1.55  
Closing the position: $1.55  

Total commission (round turn): $3.10


Performance Tab Example

Inside the Tradovate Performance report:

Gross P&L is displayed in blue.

Commission deductions are displayed in red.

Example calculation:

Trade P&L: -$65  
Commission: -$3.10  

Final Net P&L: -$68.10


NinjaTrader Users

If you are using NinjaTrader with Tradovate or Rithmic connections, commissions may not automatically appear in your balance.

You can enable commission tracking manually.

Recommended setup:

Use the "Lifetime" commission template when configuring commissions in NinjaTrader.

Tradovate Instrument List

Only the instruments listed in the Tradovate instrument list are supported.

If you attempt to trade an unsupported instrument, the order will be rejected with no penalties.


Additional Instrument Notes

Some instruments may require additional exchange subscriptions.

ICE US products are no longer supported as of March 13, 2024.


Cryptocurrency Contract Note

Micro cryptocurrency products behave like mini contracts when calculating maximum contract limits.

Example:

If your account allows:

100 micro contracts  
10 mini contracts  

You can only trade 10 contracts of:

MBT (Micro Bitcoin)  
MET (Micro Ether)


Final Notes

Always monitor commissions and platform balances to ensure accurate profit and loss calculations.

Check the instrument list periodically for updates regarding supported instruments and associated fees.
