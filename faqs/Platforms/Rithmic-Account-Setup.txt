Rithmic Account Setup

Overview
This guide explains how to install and configure Rithmic RTrader Pro for Apex Trader Funding accounts.

If you are already using Rithmic RTrader Pro and it is connecting correctly, you can skip this section and proceed to the NinjaTrader Setup guide.


Existing Users

If you already have Apex’s version of RTrader Pro installed and working, no additional setup is required.

You can continue directly to the NinjaTrader setup process.


New Users

If you recently purchased your first Rithmic account through Apex Trader Funding, complete the following steps before setting up NinjaTrader.


Step 1 — Uninstall Other Versions of RTrader

If you previously installed another version of RTrader:

- Completely uninstall the existing RTrader software
- Delete any related Rithmic folders on your computer

This prevents conflicts between different versions of the platform.


Step 2 — Download Rithmic RTrader Pro

Download and install the correct version of Rithmic RTrader Pro from the official website:

https://www.rithmic.com/rtraderpro


Step 3 — Locate Your Login Credentials

Your Rithmic login credentials are available in two locations:

- Your Apex Trader Funding welcome email
- Your Apex Trader Funding Member Dashboard

Inside the dashboard, look for the section labeled:

Rithmic and NinjaTrader Setup


Step 4 — Log In With Your Credentials

Copy and paste your Rithmic username and password exactly as shown.

Important:
Do not include extra spaces before or after the credentials when copying them. Extra spaces may cause login errors.


Rithmic RTrader Pro Configuration

After installing and logging into RTrader Pro, configure the platform using the following settings.


Open RTrader Pro
Launch the RTrader Pro application after installation.


Skip Initial Settings
Click “Skip Settings” in the top-right corner of the setup window.


Selective Load
Leave Selective Load turned OFF.


Enter Credentials
Copy and paste your Rithmic ID and password carefully.

Make sure there are no extra spaces in the credentials.


System Selection
Under the System field, select:

APEX


Gateway Selection
Choose the gateway server closest to your geographic location.


Market Data Setting
Leave Market Data set to OFF.

Rithmic allows only one active Market Data session at a time.

If you are logged in on another platform (such as NinjaTrader), that counts as your active session.

To use two market data sessions simultaneously, you must purchase the “Rithmic 2nd Login Session” add-on.


Login and Agreements

Click LOGIN after entering your settings.

All new users will be prompted to sign two required agreements before accessing the platform.


Market Data Classification

When prompted, choose your market data classification.

Most Apex traders should select:

NON-PROFESSIONAL


Non-Professional Data Requirements

To qualify as a non-professional market data user, you must meet the following conditions:

- You are an individual or small business using market data only for personal trading
- You are not registered or licensed with any securities, futures, or commodities regulatory authority
- You are not a member or seat holder of any exchange
- You do not work for a brokerage, bank, investment firm, or financial services company
- You do not manage or trade assets for other individuals or organizations


Next Step

After completing the Rithmic setup process, proceed to the NinjaTrader Setup guide to connect NinjaTrader to your Apex Rithmic accounts.
