Commissions for WealthCharts 
Welcome to the current commission schedule for both Evaluation and Paid/Funded Accounts. Our goal is to provide you with the lowest possible commissions to enhance your trading experience.

How Commissions Are Deducted
As you execute trades, commissions are automatically deducted from your account balance. 

Please review the image below that shows the WealthCharts Trades Panel and how commissions are deducted.

Example: A trade is placed using 1 contract on NQ. The per side commission is $1.55. The trade is opened with 1 contract and closed with a second contract to flatten the position. Hence, 2 contracts multiplied by $1.55 commission = $3.10.
 

On the WealthCharts Trades panel within the Broker Portfolio, the trade net P&L of $1.90 is shown in blue. The commissions for the trade have already been deducted. The gross P&L for a 0.25 point NQ trade is $5, then deducting $3.10 in commissions, this equates to a net P&L of $1.90.
