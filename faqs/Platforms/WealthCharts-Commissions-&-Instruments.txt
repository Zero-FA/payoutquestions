WealthCharts Commissions & Instruments

Overview
This guide explains the commission structure and supported trading instruments for Apex Trader Funding accounts using the WealthCharts platform.


How Commissions Are Deducted

Commissions are automatically deducted from your account balance when trades are executed.

Example:

A trade is placed using 1 contract on NQ.

Per-side commission: $1.55

Opening the position: $1.55  
Closing the position: $1.55  

Total commission (round turn): $3.10


Trade Panel Example

Inside the WealthCharts Trades panel within the Broker Portfolio:

Net P&L is displayed after commissions are deducted.

Example calculation:

Gross P&L for a 0.25 point NQ trade = $5.00  
Commission deducted = $3.10  

Final Net P&L = $1.90

Cryptocurrency Contract Notes

Do NOT trade the mini contracts for crypto futures.

Unsupported:

Mini Bitcoin (BT)  
Mini Ethereum (ET)


Micro crypto products count as mini contracts for contract limits.

Example:

If your account allows:

100 micro contracts  
10 mini contracts  

You may only trade:

10 contracts of MBT or MET.
