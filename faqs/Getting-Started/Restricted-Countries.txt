Restricted Countries

Overview
Apex Trader Funding allows traders from over 100 countries to participate in its programs.

However, certain countries are restricted from opening or maintaining trading accounts due to regulatory, operational, or security limitations.


Why Some Countries Are Restricted
There are several reasons why Apex Trader Funding cannot provide services in certain countries.

Sanctions and Embargoes
International laws and sanctions may restrict financial services from being offered to certain regions.

Service Provider Limitations
Some brokers, trading platforms, or data providers cannot legally operate in specific countries. Without these services, Apex cannot provide trading accounts.

Fraud and Security Risks
Countries with higher rates of fraud, identity theft, or credit card abuse present challenges for maintaining secure trading environments.

Payment Restrictions
Some countries restrict foreign credit card usage or cross-border financial transactions, which may prevent payments or account verification.

Language and Support Limitations
In some regions Apex cannot provide adequate language support or educational resources required to properly support traders.


Exceptions for Traders From Restricted Countries
Traders originally from restricted countries may still be eligible to participate if they currently live and work in an approved country.

To qualify, the following conditions must be met:

You must have a valid government-issued ID from an approved country.
Your primary residence must be located in a non-restricted country.
Your mailing address must be in a non-restricted country.
Your bank account must be based in a non-restricted country.


Restrictions While Located in a Restricted Country
If you are physically located in a restricted country, you will not be able to:

Purchase new trading accounts
Request payouts
Maintain full access to your trading accounts

These restrictions are automatically enforced and no exceptions are allowed.


Identity Verification
During registration Apex will verify your eligibility through identity documentation.

Accepted forms of identification include:

Driver’s License
Residency Permit (excluding temporary or student visas)
Passport
State ID
Physical government-issued identification card

The following forms of identification are NOT accepted:

Copies of IDs
Temporary IDs
Expired IDs
Visas alone
Student or work IDs
Digital ID images
Photos of IDs displayed on a device screen


Affiliate Availability
Even if traders cannot open trading accounts due to location restrictions, they may still be eligible to participate in the Apex Affiliate Program.

Canadian Traders
Canadian traders are allowed to use Apex Trader Funding services.

Important note:
Canadian traders cannot purchase live Tradovate accounts.

However, Apex accounts (Evaluations and Performance Accounts) are simulated accounts, so Canadian traders can still participate using Apex accounts.
