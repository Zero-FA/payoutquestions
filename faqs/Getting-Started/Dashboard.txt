Apex Trader Funding Dashboard

Overview
The Apex Trader Funding User Dashboard allows traders to manage their accounts, monitor performance, activate Performance Accounts, review trading statistics, and manage payout and affiliate settings.

This guide explains the major sections of the dashboard and what each page is used for.


Eval Charts and PA Charts
The Eval Charts and PA Charts sections provide a visual overview of trading performance and account status.

These charts and tables can be accessed from the sidebar on the left side of the dashboard.

Features of the charts include:

Cumulative Profit and Loss (PnL) Chart
Displays the change in account profit or loss over time based on trading days.

Account Metrics Table
Updated nightly at midnight ET.

Displayed metrics include:
- Most recent trade date
- Account type
- Total profit and loss
- Number of trading days completed

Risk and Performance Indicators
The dashboard displays important thresholds including:

- Trailing stop (maximum allowed drawdown)
- Current account balance
- Profit target levels
- Progress bar showing distance to targets or potential rule violations

Trailing Stop Display
The trailing stop value appears only for WealthCharts and Rithmic accounts.

For Tradovate accounts, this field will always display as $0.

Account Status Indicators
The Status and State columns show whether an account is:

- Active
- Passed

This allows traders to quickly determine the current state of their Evaluation or Performance Account.


Accounts Section

Passed Evaluations
The Passed Evals tab allows traders to activate Performance Accounts after passing an evaluation.

Inside this section you can:

- View a list of passed evaluation accounts
- Purchase the Performance Account (PA) activation
- Download the certificate for a passed evaluation

Each account entry also shows the account size and platform type (for example: 50K Rithmic).


Account Details
The Account Details section is located under the Accounts dropdown menu.

This section provides detailed information about each trading account.

To view a specific account:

1. Open the Accounts dropdown
2. Select the desired account from the list


Summary Tab
The Summary tab displays key account information including:

- Account size
- Current balance
- Total profit
- Number of trading days completed
- Payout eligibility requirements (for Performance Accounts)
- Profit target requirements (for Evaluation accounts)


Trading Details Tab
The Trading Details tab provides deeper trading analytics including:

- Trading performance chart
- Daily trading session breakdown
- Violations or adjustments applied to the account


Affiliate Program and Payout Settings

Accessing the Affiliate Program
Information about the Apex Trader Funding Affiliate Program can be accessed:

- On the Apex website
- From the Affiliate link located in the Resources section at the bottom of the Apex homepage


Affiliate Commission Payout Schedule

Monthly Reports
Affiliate commission reports are generated on the first day of each month.

Example:
Commissions earned from August 1–31 appear in the report generated on September 1.


Payment Timeline
Affiliate payouts are processed by the 15th of the month following the report generation.


Processing Time
After a payout is released, funds typically arrive within:

3–7 business days

Actual timing may vary depending on the financial institution.


Support Ticket Timing
Do not submit a support ticket regarding affiliate payouts until at least 7 business days after the payout has been released.


Minimum Affiliate Payout Requirement
Affiliate payouts require a minimum balance of $1000.

If the commission balance is below $1000, the amount rolls over to the next month until the minimum threshold is reached.

Example:

June Commission: $300 → No payout in July

July Commission: $800 → Total becomes $1100 → Paid on August 15


Payout Methods

General Guidelines
To ensure timely payouts, traders and affiliates must keep payout information accurate and up to date.


Payment Providers
Apex no longer uses Wise or DEEL for payouts.

International payouts are processed through the payment provider Plane.


Payment Form Accuracy
Ensure the payout form is filled out correctly.

Important fields include:

- Wire transfer routing numbers
- ACH routing numbers
- Complete bank account details


Company Payout Requests
If requesting payouts for a company:

Enter the full company name in the "First Name" field.

Example:
ABC Business, LLC


International Trader Requirements
Non-U.S. traders and affiliates must select the INTERNATIONAL payout option.

This helps prevent payout processing delays.


Plane Payment Processor
International traders will receive an invitation email from Plane when their first payout is processed.

This email may take up to two weeks to arrive.

Do not submit a support ticket if the invitation email has not arrived immediately.


Updating Payout Method and Address
To ensure payouts are processed correctly:

- Keep payout information up to date
- Verify payout method details
- Maintain an accurate address on file

Address changes must be submitted through a support ticket to the Apex Help Desk.
