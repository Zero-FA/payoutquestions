Prohibited Activities

Overview
To maintain integrity and ensure funding is provided only to disciplined and consistent traders, Apex Trader Funding strictly prohibits certain behaviors and trading practices.

Violating these rules may result in account closure, forfeiture of funds, or additional verification procedures.


Account Ownership and Access

Account Sharing
You may not share your account access credentials with anyone.

Only the registered account holder may access and trade the account.


Unauthorized Users
Allowing another individual to access or verify your account is prohibited.

Apex may request additional verification, audits, or documentation to ensure the authorized user is the only person accessing the account.


Multiple User Accounts
Creating multiple Apex user accounts is prohibited.

This includes:

- Creating multiple personal accounts
- Creating additional accounts within the same household
- Creating accounts to bypass restrictions such as evaluation limits or trader probation


Payment and Financial Information

Payment Methods
All payments must be made using a bank account, debit card, or credit card registered in your own name.

Reward payouts may only be sent to a bank account held in your own name.


Chargebacks and Payment Disputes
Submitting a chargeback, dispute, or refund request in bad faith is prohibited.


False Information
Submitting false or misleading information or documents is prohibited.

This includes information related to:

- Identity
- Residency
- Financial status
- Account details


Identity Concealment and Network Manipulation

VPN and Proxy Use
Using VPNs, proxy servers, cloud servers, anonymizing tools, or similar technologies to conceal identity or location is prohibited when used to:

- Circumvent rules or security controls
- Hide previous violations
- Bypass geographic restrictions
- Engage in fraudulent or deceptive activity


System Manipulation
Manipulating Apex systems, documents, trade logs, communications, or impersonating company staff is strictly prohibited.


Abuse of Services
Using Apex services in a way that attempts to exploit or “game” the system for personal advantage is prohibited.


Trading Behavior Restrictions

Stop Loss and Risk Management
All trades must have either a pending stop loss or a defined mental stop loss and risk management strategy.

Trading without risk management measures is prohibited.


High-Risk Trading Strategies
Strategies that risk large losses for very small profit targets are not allowed.

Example:
A five-tick profit target combined with a 150-tick stop loss.


Using the Drawdown Threshold as a Stop Loss
Using the account’s maximum drawdown threshold as a stop loss to absorb large losses is prohibited.


Unsustainable Trading Strategies
Any trading approach that fails to demonstrate consistent growth or responsible risk management is prohibited.


Professional Trading Standards
Traders must use strategies consistent with those used when trading personal capital with a regulated broker.

Apex does not fund traders who deviate from responsible professional trading standards.


Simulation Environment Manipulation

Manipulating the Simulated Environment
Attempting to manipulate the simulated trading environment is strictly prohibited.

This includes:

- High Frequency Trading (HFT) designed to exploit the simulation
- Attempting to obtain erroneous fills
- Violating maximum contract limits


Non-Directional Bracket Trading
Leaving orders open on both sides of the market in an attempt to capture random price movement is prohibited.


Hedging and Non-Directional Trading

No Hedging
Holding both long and short positions simultaneously on the same or correlated instruments is strictly prohibited.

Trading must be directional and based on strategy rather than hedging both sides of the market.


Trade Position Rules

Holding Positions Through Market Close
Open positions must be closed before market close.

Holding positions through the close is prohibited.


Account and Resource Sharing

Shared Devices or Infrastructure
Sharing the following with other traders is strictly prohibited:

- MAC addresses
- Computers
- IP addresses
- Credit cards
- Trade copying arrangements

Violations may result in account closure and additional verification audits.


Evaluation Account Abuse

Stockpiling Evaluations
Purchasing large numbers of discounted evaluation accounts with the intent of cycling through accounts or intentionally failing them for windfall profits is prohibited.


Automation and Algorithm Use

No Automated Trading Systems
Automated trading systems, algorithms, or preprogrammed logic are not permitted.

Rewards are intended for human traders actively participating in the learning and trading process.


News Trading

Permitted
Trading during news events as part of a normal trading strategy is allowed.

Prohibited
Strategies that attempt to gamble on news events by placing orders on both sides of the market are not allowed.


Consequences of Violations
If prohibited activities are detected:

- Accounts may be closed
- Account balances may be forfeited
- Apex may conduct further audits or verification procedures

These measures ensure compliance with the Apex Trader Funding trader agreement.


Compliance Expectations
Traders are expected to follow these rules to maintain accounts in good standing and demonstrate the consistent and disciplined trading practices required for funding.
