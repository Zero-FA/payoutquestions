Payment Methods and Possible Checkout Errors
Important Note
All purchases must be made using a card that is registered under your own name. Payments made with cards belonging to someone else are not allowed.

Accepted Payment Methods
Apex Trader Funding supports the following payment options for all transactions:

Credit & Debit Cards
Visa

Mastercard

Discover

Both credit and debit versions of these cards are accepted, as long as they are issued in your name.

Unsupported Payment Methods
Gift cards

American Express cards.

Crypto

These forms of payment are not accepted.

Possible Checkout Errors
Declined or failed payments can happen for a variety of reasons, including bank security measures, incorrect billing details, or retrying the payment too quickly. Below are the most common issues and how to resolve them.

Error 609 — Transaction Blocked by Issuer
This error usually means that your bank has flagged or blocked the transaction, often for security or fraud-prevention reasons.

What to Do
Contact your bank or card issuer and ask them to approve or authorize the charge.

Confirm that your card is allowed to process:

Online transactions

International transactions (some banks block these by default)

Billing Information Errors
Even small mismatches in your billing information can cause payments to fail. Make sure the following details match exactly what appears on your bank statement:

Full name

Billing address

Country

ZIP/Postal code

Card number, expiration date, and CVV

Retrying the Payment
After your bank confirms everything is authorized, retry the payment from your Apex Dashboard.

If the issue continues after contacting your bank, try updating the payment method with a different card to avoid further delays.

Avoiding Duplicate Payment Errors
This issue normally appears when:

You attempt the same payment too quickly after a failed attempt.

Multiple retries are submitted within a short period of time.

How to Prevent It:

Wait 2–5 minutes before attempting the payment again.

Refresh your Apex Dashboard before retrying.

Do not click the checkout button multiple times during the same attempt.
