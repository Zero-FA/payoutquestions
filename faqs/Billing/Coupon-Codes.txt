Coupon Codes

Overview
Coupon codes can be applied during checkout when purchasing an account. Discounts must be applied before completing payment. Once payment has been processed, coupon codes cannot be added retroactively.

Applying a Coupon Code
Coupons must be entered during checkout when making your purchase.

Steps:
1. Enter the coupon code you were given, or use SAVENOW if you do not have one.
2. Agree to the Trading Rules and policies.
3. Click Next to proceed.
4. Review the checkout page to confirm the discount has been applied.
5. Enter your credit card information and complete the purchase.

Missed Coupons or Discounts
If a coupon code was not applied before payment is completed:

- The coupon cannot be applied retroactively.
- Refunds or adjustments for missed discounts will not be provided.

To avoid this issue, carefully review the order summary and discount amount before completing checkout.

Evaluation Discount Rules
Evaluation discounts are only valid for accounts purchased during the sale period.

PA Activation Fee Discount Rules
Discounted Performance Account (PA) activation fees are only valid through the promotion date listed.

Important details:
- After the promotion date, normal PA activation fees apply.
- The PA activation discount does NOT remain attached to an evaluation account just because the evaluation was purchased during the sale.
- PA activation fee discounts can be used on existing evaluations purchased before the sale, not only new accounts.
- Once the promotion period ends, the activation fee returns to the regular price.
