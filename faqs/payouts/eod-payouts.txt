EOD Payouts

Overview
End-of-Day (EOD) Performance Accounts allow traders to request payouts once certain trading and profit requirements are met. Approved payouts are issued with a 100% payout split, meaning you receive the full approved payout amount.

EOD Payouts at a Glance
• Up to weekly payouts available
• 100% payout split
• Minimum 5 qualifying trading days
• Minimum payout amount: $500
• 50% consistency rule applies
• Maximum of 6 payouts per Performance Account

Payout eligibility depends on account size, account type, trading activity, balance thresholds, and consistency requirements.

Minimum Trading Days
Each account must complete at least five qualifying trading days before requesting a payout.

A qualifying trading day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for that account size.

These days do not need to be consecutive.

Example:
On a 25K EOD Performance Account, you must have at least five trading days where each day produces $100 or more in net profit.

The earliest payout request can occur after five qualifying trading days, which allows weekly payouts.

There is no deadline to complete the qualifying days.


Minimum Daily Profit Requirement
Each qualifying trading day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for the account size.

Example:
For a 50K EOD Performance Account:

• At least five trading days must each generate $250 or more in profit.
• Only days meeting the minimum amount count toward the five-day requirement.


50% Consistency Rule
No single profitable trading day may represent 50% or more of the total profit earned since your last approved payout.

If a single day exceeds this threshold, the payout request option will not appear until additional trading reduces the percentage below 50%.

The account remains active during this time.


Safety Net Requirement
The Safety Net is calculated as:

Maximum Drawdown Limit + $100

This level must be maintained for the lifetime of the Performance Account.

Only profits above the Safety Net are eligible for payout.


Minimum Payout Amount
The minimum payout request is $500 for all account sizes.

The "Minimum Balance to Request" shown in the payout table already includes:

• Starting balance
• Safety Net requirement
• Minimum payout amount ($500)

If your balance is below that threshold, the payout option will not appear.


Maximum Payouts Per Account
Each Performance Account can receive a maximum of six payouts.

After the sixth payout is approved, the Performance Account completes its payout cycle and is closed.

To obtain another Performance Account, you must pass a new evaluation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I request a payout?
The earliest payout request can be submitted after completing five qualifying trading days that meet the minimum daily profit requirement.


Do the five trading days need to be consecutive?
No. The five qualifying days can occur at any time and do not need to be consecutive.


Does each qualifying day need the same profit amount?
Each qualifying day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for that account size.

Example:
For a $25K account, each qualifying day must produce at least $100 in net profit.


Is there a deadline to complete the payout requirements?
No. There is no expiration date for completing the five qualifying trading days as long as the account remains active.


What is the minimum payout amount?
The minimum payout amount is $500 per request for all account sizes.


What is the Safety Net?
The Safety Net equals the account’s drawdown limit plus $100.

Your account balance must remain above this threshold to request payouts.


What is the 50% consistency rule?
The 50% consistency rule ensures that no single trading day represents 50% or more of total profit since the last payout.

If the rule is violated, the payout request option will not appear until the profit distribution becomes more balanced.


Can I continue trading after submitting a payout request?
Yes. You may continue trading immediately.

However, you should trade as if the payout amount has already been removed from the balance. If the balance falls below the required threshold after submitting the request, the payout will be denied.


What happens if a payout request is denied?
If a payout request is denied due to unmet requirements, the account remains active.

You may continue trading and submit a new payout request once eligibility requirements are satisfied.


How many payouts can I receive?
Each Performance Account may receive a maximum of six approved payouts.
