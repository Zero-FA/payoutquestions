How to Request a Payout

Overview
This guide explains how to request a payout from your Apex Trader Funding Performance Account (PA), where to locate the payout request option, and what happens after submitting the request.


Before Requesting a Payout

Before submitting a payout request, confirm the following:

• Your payout method and personal information are updated in your dashboard.
• You are requesting the payout from the correct Performance Account.
• Your account meets all payout eligibility requirements.

You can verify eligibility by opening the Account Details page in your dashboard. All payout eligibility criteria will be displayed there.


Accessing the Payout Request

Step 1 — Open Account Details
Log into your Apex Trader Funding dashboard.

Navigate to:

Account Details


Step 2 — Open the Payout Tab
Within the Account Details page, select the:

Payout tab


Step 3 — Select the Correct Performance Account
Choose the specific Performance Account (PA) you want to request the payout from.

If the account meets all eligibility requirements, you will see the message:

"ELIGIBLE FOR PAYOUT"

A "Request Payout" link will also appear.


Submitting the Payout Request

Step 4 — Click Request Payout
Click the "Request Payout" link to open the payout request window.

Step 5 — Complete the Request Form
Inside the payout request popup:

• Enter the payout amount you want to request
• Review the payout request agreement
• Check the box confirming agreement with the payout terms
• Click Submit


Step 6 — Confirmation Message
If the request is submitted successfully, a confirmation message will appear stating:

"Payout request submitted successfully"


Checking Payout Status

To view the status of your payout request:

Scroll down to the:

Payout History section

within the Account Details page.

This section displays the history and status of all payout requests submitted for the account.


After Submitting a Payout Request

You may continue trading immediately after submitting a payout request.

Trading does not need to stop while the payout request is being reviewed.

However, you should trade as if the payout amount has already been removed from your account balance.

This ensures your balance remains above the minimum required threshold.


Important Balance Warning

If your account balance falls below the required minimum balance after submitting a payout request:

• The payout request will be automatically denied.

You do not need to cancel the request manually.

If denied, you may continue trading and submit another payout request once your account meets the eligibility requirements again.


Common Issues and Quick Fixes

Issue: The "Request Payout" button is not visible

Possible causes include:

• Your account is not currently eligible for a payout
• The account selected is not a Performance Account
• You are within a restricted period based on payout rules

If the payout option is not visible, review the eligibility criteria shown in the Account Details page.
