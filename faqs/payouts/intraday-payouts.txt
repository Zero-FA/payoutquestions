Intraday Trailing Drawdown Payouts

Overview
Intraday Performance Accounts (PA) allow traders to request payouts once specific trading and profit requirements are met. Approved payouts are issued with a 100% payout split, meaning the trader receives the full approved payout amount.

Intraday PA Payouts at a Glance
• Up to weekly payouts available
• 100% payout split
• Minimum 5 qualifying trading days
• Minimum payout amount: $500
• 50% consistency rule applies
• Maximum of 6 payouts per Performance Account

Payout eligibility depends on account size, account type, trading activity, balance thresholds, and consistency requirements.

Minimum Trading Days
Each account must complete at least five qualifying trading days before a payout can be requested.

A qualifying trading day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for that account size.

These trading days do not need to be consecutive.

Example:
For a 25K Intraday Performance Account, at least five separate trading days must each produce $100 or more in net profit.

The earliest payout request can occur after five qualifying trading days, allowing payouts as early as once per week.

There is no deadline to complete these trading days.


Minimum Daily Profit Requirement
Each qualifying trading day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for the account size.

Example:
For a 50K Intraday Performance Account:

• At least five trading days must each generate $200 or more in net profit.
• Only days meeting the minimum profit amount count toward the five-day requirement.


50% Consistency Rule
The 50% consistency rule requires that no single profitable trading day represents 50% or more of the total profit earned since your last approved payout.

If one day exceeds this threshold, the payout request option will not appear until additional trading reduces the percentage below 50%.

The account remains active during this time.


Safety Net Requirement
The Safety Net is calculated as:

Maximum Drawdown Limit + $100

Your account balance must remain above this threshold to request payouts.

Only profits above the Safety Net are eligible to be withdrawn.


Minimum Payout Amount
The minimum payout request is $500 for all account sizes.

The “Minimum Balance to Request” shown in the payout table already includes:

• Starting account balance
• Safety Net requirement
• Minimum payout amount ($500)

If the account balance is below the minimum threshold, the payout option will not appear.


Maximum Payouts Per Account
Each Intraday Performance Account can receive a maximum of six payouts.

After the sixth payout is approved, the Performance Account completes its payout cycle and is closed.

To obtain another Performance Account, you must pass a new evaluation.

After Submitting a Payout Request
You may continue trading immediately after submitting a payout request.

Trading does not need to stop while the payout request is under review.

However, you should trade as if the payout amount has already been removed from your account balance.

If your account balance falls below the required minimum after submitting the payout request, the request will be automatically denied.

You do not need to cancel or modify the request manually.


Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I request a payout?
The earliest payout request can be submitted after completing five qualifying trading days that meet the minimum daily profit requirement.


Do the five trading days need to be consecutive?
No. The five qualifying trading days do not need to occur consecutively.


Does each qualifying day need the same profit amount?
Each qualifying day must meet the minimum daily profit requirement for that account size.

Example:
For a 25K account, each qualifying day must produce at least $100 in net profit.


Is there a deadline to complete the payout requirements?
No. There is no expiration date for completing the five qualifying trading days as long as the account remains active.


What is the minimum payout amount?
The minimum payout amount is $500 per request for all account sizes.


What is the Safety Net?
The Safety Net equals the account’s drawdown limit plus $100.

Your account balance must remain above this threshold to request payouts.


What is the 50% consistency rule?
The 50% consistency rule ensures that no single trading day accounts for 50% or more of total profits since the last approved payout.

If the rule is violated, the payout request option will not appear until the profit distribution becomes more balanced.


Can I continue trading after submitting a payout request?
Yes. You may continue trading immediately.

However, you should trade as if the payout amount has already been removed from the balance to ensure eligibility remains intact.


What happens if a payout request is denied?
If a payout request is denied due to unmet requirements, the account remains active.

You may continue trading and submit a new payout request once eligibility requirements are satisfied again.


How many payouts can I receive?
Each Performance Account may receive a maximum of six approved payouts.

After the sixth payout, the account completes its payout cycle.
